About

A mechanical engineer with good taste.

I studied mechanical engineering because I love the moment a sketch turns into something you can hold. These days I specialize in prototyping and validation — the part where ideas get punched in the face by physics and either hold up or get redesigned.

I'm happiest with a caliper in one hand and a test plan in the other. Outside of CAD, you'll find me tinkering with 3D-printed gifts for friends and over-engineering my houseplants' watering schedule.
