About

A mechanical engineer with good taste.

I studied mechanical engineering at Northeastern because I love the moment a sketch turns into something you can hold. These days I specialize in prototyping, validation, and the documentation that makes it all traceable — the part where ideas get punched in the face by physics and either hold up or get redesigned.

I've spent three co-ops at Keurig Dr Pepper, SharkNinja, and Teledyne FLIR — authoring validation reports, running DoE campaigns, performing tolerance stack-ups, and tearing down prototypes to find the real root cause. I'm happiest with a caliper in one hand and a test plan in the other.
